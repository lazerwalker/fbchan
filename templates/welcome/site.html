FBchan helps you connect and share with the people in your life.

By making Facebook look like 4chan.

FBChan

FBchan is a web-based Facebook client that lets you browse and interact with your news stream as if it were Twitter, 4chan, or another social network. It presents an online space that is simultaneously familiar and unsettling.

To get started, you'll need to authenticate with Facebook Connect.

FBchan is completely powered by your web browser, so your private data never touches our servers.
